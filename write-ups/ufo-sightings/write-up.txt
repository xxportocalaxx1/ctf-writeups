Challenge: ufo sights
Category: OSInt

Description
We are given an image showing a supposed UFO sighting and must determine the exact geographic coordinates where the photo was taken.

Solve:
First, I used google lens reverse image search to find information about the image provided.
I found a rddit post asking about the ufo design.
Someone told him it's a Mattoni eagle ad on the highway.
Next, I searched on google maps "Mattoni eagle ufo" and I found its exact location and coordinates.
